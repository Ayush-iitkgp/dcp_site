Stanford Disciplined Convex Programming Website

This website is designed to teach disciplined convex programming (DCP). DCP is a system for constructing mathematical expressions with known curvature from a given library of base functions. DCP is used by the convex optimization modeling languages CVX and CVXPY to ensure that the specified optimization problems are convex.

Visit the DCP Quiz to practice applying the DCP rules to mathematical expressions. Alternatively, visit the DCP Analyzer to type in expressions, equalities, and inequalities and see a visualization of the DCP analysis.
